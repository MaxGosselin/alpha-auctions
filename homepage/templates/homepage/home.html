How it works

On our platform: Alpha Auctions, registered teams will be able to post and bid on auctions for both X and Y, as well as Patents, Licenses, and Plants/Factories.

With all auctions displayed in real time with transparent pricing, participants can confidently bid on the products they wish to acquire. Our website is user friendly, simply register and the bidding begins!
